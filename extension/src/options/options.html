You can modify your privacy settings on when to alert you that there is a conflict.

You can modify your "Retention" levels which include the following values:

  • No Retention: This will alert you if the website retains the data for any longer than neccessary
  • Legal Retention: This will alert you if the website retains data for longer than legally required
  • Any Retentions: This option means you do not care how long your data is retained and we will never alert you

You can modify whether or not you want your data shared. By ticking the box "Share Information" we will NOT alert you if the data is shared
